About Us

MAJOKS Clinic

...Quality and affordable healthcare

MAJOKS Clinic began operations in May 2017, with the sole aim of providing quality healthcare by bridging the gap between the rich and the poor.

MAJOKS Clinic is situated at 16, Logunleko street, Cele bus stop, Ijede road, Ikorodu Lagos. Our facility is equipped with State of the Art medical equipment and qualified staffs. We are committed to providing you with quality health care at affordable price. MAJOKS Clinic is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and rightly approved by the Health Facility Monitoring and Accreditaion Agency (HEFAMAA), Lagos.

Our Vision
To provide quality healthcare to our clients at affordable prices.

Our Mission
To contribute efficiently to the growth of healthcare service delivery to the poor and middle-class
